About Us


Established and founded in 2021

Food Lovers Always Have A doubt Regarding the hygiene and quality of food they order. So the founders of the company came up with a solution to this problem as "À la carte chef".
This platform an idea of connecting the local chefs and the elite chefs with a social platform called " Chef à la carte ". Foodies can now hire a chef for a day or couple of hours according to their needs and can enjoy the delicious food that is made at their home and that too by a professional. Goodbye to the hygiene problems and goodbye to the limited choices of food.

What's Unique About Us?

1. Building employment and providing exposure even to local and underrated chefs, hence bringing all the professionals on one platform.
2. Our subsription plan for hiring the elite chefs.
3. Our Reviewing System of the chefs and rating system for the food made.

 Terms and Conditions.

1. Once subscription is taken by the consumer no refund will be provided under any circumstances.
2. Any misbehave or not following the code of conduct will result in the strict action against the violater.
3. If the chef has been hired and he/she fails to show up to the customer a full refund will be initiated under 1-2 buisiness days.
4. If the specialized chefs are asked to make any other dish rather than their specialzed one extra charge per dish will be taken by the company.

Safety Measures to be taken ny the customers and the chefs.

1. Chef's Current temperature will be shown at the time of hiring him/her and chef will be given a new N-95 mask every time he is hired to ensure protection against Covid-19.
2. Customer and Chefs should avoid unnecssary touches.
3. Proper Sanitization should be done of the chef at the time of arrival for extra safety.
4. All the cuisines will be made by the chef wearing gloves <
